# Ritual: Satisfaction Review (monthly, 30 min — pairs with metrics review)

Data: journey micro-surveys (1-5 per completion) + monthly NPS prompt (ADR-0011 etiquette:
rare, one-tap, never nagging — a poisoned survey signal is worse than none).

## Review

1. Response rate — below ~30%? The ask is mistimed or overused; fix etiquette before reading
   the numbers.
2. Score by journey (scaffold / onboard / promote) — trend, not absolutes.
3. NPS = %promoters − %detractors (criteria #8). Track direction.
4. Worst journey → one discovery conversation scheduled specifically on it.
5. Share one verbatim positive in #platform-help (criteria #7: marketing is platform work) —
   with permission.
