# 0010 — APEX facilitates the product operating model, not just the technical golden path

## Status

Accepted (2026-07-16)

## Context

The kit has two layers: the product operating model (week-zero assessment, value proposition
canvas, personas, TVP worksheet, discovery cadence, adoption/satisfaction metrics) and the
technical TVP. The question: how is layer 1 delivered? Options: human-facilitated workshops with
markdown templates; agent-facilitated throughout; or hybrid (human week-zero, agent thereafter).

## Decision

**APEX facilitates layer 1 as well.** Equipped with a platform-as-product skill (built from this
kit's criteria framework), APEX interviews the Platform Team through the week-zero assessment,
co-fills the canvas/persona/TVP templates, and later runs the recurring product rituals:
discovery interview preparation, adoption/satisfaction metric reviews, prioritization prep.

One front door for both layers — the same agent that scaffolds a service also coaches the product
mindset. The kit eats its own dog food: its thought-leadership layer is itself delivered as a
curated, versioned agent experience.

## Consequences

- The product operating model becomes executable, not shelf-ware: rituals have an agent that
  initiates and structures them, which is what a team new to product thinking most lacks.
- The kit's differentiation sharpens: "agentic platform engineering" applies to the operating
  model, not just infrastructure scaffolding.
- Human facilitation (you / the container specialist team) remains available in the guided-first
  engagement, but the artifacts and rituals do not depend on a facilitator being present —
  supporting the self-serve afterlife of the engagement.
- Requires authoring a first-party platform-product skill from the criteria framework in
  docs/context/ — this is now a core kit deliverable, on par with the KRO types and app
  templates.
- Risk accepted: senior-stakeholder trust-building may still need human presence in week zero;
  the guided engagement covers this without changing the artifact design.
